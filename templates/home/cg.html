Community Guidelines

Building a safe, supportive space for everyone to heal and grow together

Our Promise to You

EmotionSpace is more than just a platform—it's a sanctuary for healing, growth, and authentic connection. These guidelines help us maintain a space where vulnerability is honored, pain is acknowledged, and hope is nurtured. Every interaction here has the power to heal or harm, so we choose healing, always.

🤝

Respect & Kindness

Practice Empathy

Remember that behind every confession is a real person with real feelings. Respond with the same compassion you'd want to receive during your most vulnerable moments.

Honor Different Perspectives

We all come from different backgrounds and experiences. What might seem insignificant to you could be life-changing for someone else. Validate, don't minimize.

Use Supportive Language

Choose words that uplift and encourage. Avoid judgment, criticism, or dismissive comments. When in doubt, lead with "I hear you" or "You're not alone."

🔒

Anonymous & Safe

Protect Anonymity

Never attempt to identify or expose someone's real identity. Don't share personal details that could compromise someone's anonymity, including your own.

Keep Confessions Here

What's shared in EmotionSpace stays in EmotionSpace. Don't screenshot, share, or discuss confessions outside this platform. Trust is sacred.

Report Concerning Content

If you see content that suggests immediate harm to self or others, please report it immediately so we can connect the person with appropriate resources.

✨

Authentic Sharing

Share Your Truth

This is your space to be completely honest about your feelings, experiences, and struggles. Authenticity creates deeper connections and healing.

It's Okay to Not Be Okay

You don't need to have everything figured out or provide solutions. Sometimes just naming your pain and being witnessed is enough.

Share at Your Own Pace

There's no pressure to share more than you're comfortable with. Honor your own boundaries and emotional capacity.

⚠️

Community Boundaries

No Hate or Discrimination

Content that promotes hatred, discrimination, or harassment based on race, religion, gender, sexuality, or any other characteristic will result in immediate removal and account suspension.

No Harmful Advice

Don't provide medical, legal, or professional advice unless you're qualified to do so. Instead, encourage seeking appropriate professional help when needed.

No Explicit Content

While we welcome discussions about sexuality and relationships, explicit sexual content, graphic violence, or disturbing imagery is not allowed.

No Self-Promotion

This space is for genuine emotional sharing, not advertising, promoting services, or building personal brands. Keep the focus on healing and support.

🧠

Mental Health & Wellness

Encourage Professional Help

If someone is struggling with serious mental health issues, gently encourage them to seek professional support while still offering emotional validation.

Share Resources Thoughtfully

When sharing mental health resources, make sure they're from reputable sources. Personal recommendations should be clearly labeled as such.

Recognize Your Limits

You're not responsible for "fixing" anyone. Sometimes the most helpful thing you can do is listen and remind someone they're not alone.

Community Accountability

Progressive Responses

Violations may result in content removal, warnings, temporary restrictions, or permanent account suspension, depending on severity and frequency.

Appeal Process

If you believe action was taken in error, you can appeal through our support team. We're committed to fair and transparent moderation.

Community Self-Moderation

We encourage the community to gently redirect conversations that aren't aligned with our values. Sometimes peer guidance is more effective than moderation.

We're Here to Help

Have questions about these guidelines or need support? Our team is committed to maintaining this safe space for everyone.
